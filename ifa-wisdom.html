IFA Wisdom

Ancient divination system and spiritual guidance of the Yoruba people

What is IFA?

IFA is a comprehensive divination system and spiritual tradition that has guided the Yoruba people for millennia. It represents a vast body of knowledge encompassing philosophy, medicine, psychology, and cosmology. Through IFA divination, practitioners (Babalawos) access divine wisdom to provide guidance on life's challenges and opportunities.

The IFA literary corpus, known as the Odu IFA, consists of 256 chapters (Odu) containing thousands of verses. These verses encode the collective wisdom of countless generations, offering insights into every aspect of human existence.
